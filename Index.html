Typing Challenge Game!

Description!

The game will quickly start then your on the clock!You will have a limited amount of time to start typing the
green letter that appears on screen. Your score will go up by +10 as you get on correct and if you get one wrong
you will go down -10. The goal of the game is to get the highest score possible and try to beat your friends.
